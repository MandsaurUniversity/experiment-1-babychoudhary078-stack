About the Department

The Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) department offers a 3-year undergraduate program focused on software development, computer applications, and IT skills. Typically requiring 12th-grade completion with 50% marks (often including mathematics), the curriculum covers programming (C, C++, Java), database management, networking, and web development.The curriculum is constantly updated to keep pace with industry requirements, fostering analytical thinking, technical expertise, and problem-solving abilities in students. Beyond technical training, the department encourages overall personality development through seminars, technical quizzes, and group projects to ensure graduates are industry-ready.
